Our Story

The Disco Manifesto

We believe hair appointments should be the highlight of your week. Here's why.

We Believe In

Hair. Music.
Good Times.

Disco Cuts was born from a simple idea: getting your hair done should feel amazing — not just the result, but the whole experience.

We set out to create a space where artistry meets atmosphere. Where you walk in feeling like yourself and walk out feeling like the best version of yourself. Where the playlist is always on point, the stylists actually listen, and the vibe is genuinely, unapologetically fun.

Seattle deserved a salon that was as dynamic and spirited as the city itself. So we built Disco Cuts — and we named everything in it to match the energy we bring every single day.
